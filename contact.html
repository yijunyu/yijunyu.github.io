For Prospective Students

If you are interested in pursuing a PhD or research collaboration, please send me an email with:

  • Your CV
  • A brief description of your research interests
  • Why you are interested in working with me
  • Any relevant publications or projects

I particularly welcome applications in the areas of automated software engineering, deep learning for code, program transformation, and requirements engineering.

For Industry Partners

I have experience collaborating with industry partners including NATS, Huawei, IBM, CA, and RealTelekom on knowledge transfer projects. If you are interested in research collaboration or consultancy, please get in touch.
